What is DeadTime?

DeadTime lets you record heartfelt messages for your loved ones that will be delivered automatically after you’re gone. Create video, audio or written notes that will live on in a secure vault until it’s time to send them.

Why DeadTime?

  • Peace of mind: know your final words will be heard.
  • Emotional closure: share memories, wisdom and love.
  • Legacy: leave a part of yourself for future generations.
  • Privacy: your messages are encrypted and kept safe until delivery.

How It Works

  1. Create your vault: sign up and start recording video, audio or text messages.
  2. Choose recipients: decide who will receive each message.
  3. Set the trigger: choose a period of inactivity or verified passing.
  4. Your messages delivered: our system releases your messages when the time is right.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is DeadTime secure?

Yes. All messages are encrypted and stored securely. Only your chosen recipients can access them when it’s time.

How do you know when to send my messages?

You set the trigger. We monitor inactivity or other signals and verify before delivering anything.

Can I update or delete my messages?

Absolutely. You can edit or remove any message at any time while you have access to your account.
