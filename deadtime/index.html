What is DeadTime?

DeadTime allows you to record messages for your loved ones that will be delivered automatically after you’re gone. Create video, audio, or written notes that will be sent to the people you choose.

Features

  • Secure message vault with encryption
  • Video, audio and text messages
  • Flexible delivery triggers
  • Customizable recipients

Pricing

Free: one text message
Premium: unlimited messages and video/audio recordings ($4.99/month)
