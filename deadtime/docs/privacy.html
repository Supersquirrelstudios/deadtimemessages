Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24 August 2025

DeadTime (“we,” “our,” or “us”) provides a platform for recording, scheduling, and delivering messages to loved ones. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use, store and protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password (stored as a hash). We do not collect unnecessary information.
  • Message Content: You may upload video, audio or text messages. These are encrypted and stored securely in our cloud storage provider until delivered.
  • Recipient Details: Names and email addresses (or phone numbers) of your recipients so that we can deliver your messages.
  • Usage Data: We collect basic analytics (e.g. last login date) to determine inactivity and operate the service. We do not track your activity for marketing purposes.

2. Legal Basis and Consent

We only process your personal data when we have a lawful basis to do so. Our primary legal bases are contractual necessity (to provide the service), legitimate interests (to ensure security and functionality), and your explicit consent. In accordance with data protection laws such as the GDPR, consent must be freely given, specific, informed and unambiguous【32231640842400†L104-L114】. You may withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your account or contacting us. When you withdraw consent, we stop collecting and processing your data【32231640842400†L104-L114】.

3. Data Minimization

We follow the principle of data minimization by collecting only the minimum amount of information necessary to provide our service【937969418581650†L123-L129】. We do not request sensitive personal data such as race, gender, or health information. If you choose to include such information in your messages, you acknowledge that you are voluntarily providing it.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain and improve the DeadTime service;
  • Store and schedule your messages for future delivery;
  • Authenticate your account and send confirmation emails (e.g. to notify you of impending inactivity triggers);
  • Comply with our legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service;
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.

5. Data Storage, Encryption & Backup

Your messages are stored in encrypted form in our cloud storage provider. Sensitive files are encrypted before being saved and we use industry-standard encryption protocols (AES-256) to protect data in transit and at rest. We follow the 3‑2‑1 backup rule — three copies of important files, on two different types of media, with one stored off‑site【35789964984834†L262-L269】. We also encourage you to test your backups regularly【35789964984834†L262-L269】.

6. Third‑Party Services

We use third‑party service providers for hosting (e.g., AWS), email delivery and payment processing. These providers may process your data only to the extent necessary to perform their services and are bound by strict confidentiality and security obligations. We do not sell or rent your personal data.

7. International Transfers

If you access our services from outside the United States, please be aware that your data may be transferred to, stored and processed in the United States. We implement safeguards to ensure an adequate level of protection for personal data transferred internationally.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data;
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data where legally permitted;
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (note that this may affect your ability to use the service);
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority in your jurisdiction.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter to comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes. Messages scheduled for delivery are retained until delivered or deleted by you.

10. Children’s Privacy

DeadTime is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through a notice on our website. The updated policy will be effective when posted.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests regarding this policy, please contact us at privacy@deadtimelife.com.
